## Company Snapshot

Honeywell is a diversified industrial technology company headquartered in Charlotte, NC,
operating across aerospace, building automation, energy and sustainability, and industrial
automation (including Honeywell Intelligrated warehouse automation and Honeywell Connected
software). Publicly traded (NASDAQ: HON), it is a large, multi-segment manufacturer.
Business model: industrial products + aftermarket services + connected software.

## Supply Chain & Ops Profile

- **Stack:** enterprise SAP environment typical of a large industrial (ERP backbone across
  segments), with Honeywell Connected and Forge as its own industrial software layer.
- **Logistics model:** global manufacturing footprint, complex multi-tier supplier base,
  significant spare-parts and aftermarket operations.
- **Profile:** high-SKU, long-lifecycle products; aftermarket and spare-parts planning is a
  perennial complexity.

## Pain Points & Signals

- Active portfolio reshaping and announced business separations signal ERP/system
  disentanglement work and carve-out complexity — fertile ground for planning and data tooling.
- Aerospace and industrial supply chains remain exposed to component lead-time volatility
  and supplier risk — a standing operational pain.
- Aftermarket / spare-parts planning across a vast installed base is a classic
  inventory-optimization problem at scale.
