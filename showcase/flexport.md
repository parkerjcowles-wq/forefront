## Company Snapshot
Flexport is a technology-enabled global freight forwarding and supply chain management platform founded in 2013 by Ryan Petersen in San Francisco, California, operating as a private company with backing from investors including Andreessen Horowitz, Founders Fund, and SoftBank. As of April 2026 (verify), Flexport generated approximately $2.1 billion in revenue.

## Supply Chain & Ops Profile
Flexport's software stack includes Anaplan for supply chain planning. The company operates a freight-to-fulfillment platform, combining five U.S. warehouses and the former Deliverr fast-shipping network with Flexport's freight-forwarding platform. Flexport's logistics model is a hybrid of in-house and 3PL services.
