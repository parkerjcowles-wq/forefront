## Company Snapshot

project44 is a Chicago-based supply chain technology company whose Movement platform
delivers real-time transportation visibility (RTTVP) across road, ocean, air, rail, and
parcel. It is venture-backed (raised at a multi-billion-dollar valuation) and serves
shippers, carriers, and logistics service providers globally. Business model: B2B SaaS,
sold per shipment volume and network connections.

## Supply Chain & Ops Profile

- **Stack:** their own product is the stack — the Movement visibility platform, expanded
  through acquisitions (Convey for last-mile/returns, Ocean Insights for ocean tracking).
- **Logistics model:** asset-light software; value is the carrier network and data graph.
- **Profile:** data-and-integration heavy — thousands of carrier API/EDI connections;
  growth depends on network density and data quality.

## Pain Points & Signals

- Intense competitive pressure in the visibility category from FourKites, Tive, and
  Overhaul — differentiation and net-revenue retention are the pressure points.
- A softer freight market since 2023 squeezes shipper IT budgets, lengthening sales cycles
  for visibility tooling — relevant to anything sold *into* their go-to-market.
- Heavy push into AI ("Movement" + agentic features) signals investment in data tooling,
  ML infrastructure, and engineering talent.
